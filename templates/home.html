What is ProjectGPT?

ProjectGPT is a web app that uses advanced AI to help users create personalized storybooks with text, images, and even audio narration. Designed for students, educators, and storytellers of all kinds.

Why It Matters

By merging creativity with technology, ProjectGPT opens a world of storytelling possibilities. Whether for learning, publishing, or fun, your imagination is the only limit.

Powerful Features

AI Story Generator

Generate unique stories in seconds from your input—perfect for learning, fun, or publishing.

Visual Illustrations

Each story page includes matching illustrations generated by advanced image models.

Voice Narration

Convert your stories into narrated audio, ideal for accessibility and engagement.
