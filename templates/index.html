What is ProjectGPT+?

Project GPT+ is a web-based educational initiative that leverages generative AI to make complex subjects accessible and engaging for young learners. By fine-tuning a GPT model, the platform creates personalized, interactive storybooks based on students’ questions or topics of interest, followed by quizzes that help learners evaluate and reinforce their understanding.

Why It Matters

In many underserved schools, students face a serious barrier: a lack of access to quality educational materials. Without textbooks, digital tools, or engaging content, it’s difficult for children to fully understand the subjects that shape their futures. Project GPT+ responds to this challenge by using AI to generate personalized, illustrated storybooks and quizzes — making complex topics like science, business, and the environment accessible, interactive, and engaging even in low-resource settings.

Our First Client

Malabuyoc Central Elementary School

Located in Cebu Province of the Philippines, Malabuyoc Central Elementary School has about 250 students enrolled. The school faces different barriers concerning the availability of educational materials. These barriers, including a lack of textbooks and other resources, have made it increasingly difficult for students to attain a comprehensive education in our modern society, particularly in an era where technology is prevalent.

Recognizing these challenges, we are committed to supporting solutions that empower both students and educators in Malabuyoc.
